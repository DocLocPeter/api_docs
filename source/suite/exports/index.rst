Exporting  Contact and Email Data
=================================

Lists contacts according to the method provided, then transports them into a CSV file. This is either uploaded to an FTP or stored on a local host.
In the case of local storage, data is on an emarsys server, which is available for the customer via WebDav connection. WebDav connections must be enabled, please
contact emarsys support for more information.
